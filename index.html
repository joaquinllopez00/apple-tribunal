for apple.

my tribute to apple.

for all the times their innovations has made my life a little bit better

everyday life - but easier.

When the original iPhone was released, I was 7 years old. Too young to even watch a PG-13 movie without a parent, yet I still heard about the device that broke into the cellphone market and changed the expectations for a cellphone. Four years later I saved my money and bought my self an iPod touch - starting my long relationship with Apple's products.

everyday work - but faster.

I was never afforded a Mac until I was 17 and had saved enough money to buy one for myself, and after a few months of power-using - it thrusted me further into my commitment to Apple and the unmatched productivity I found when using their products.

everyday listening - but with atmosphere.

Growing up, I was always an avid music listener. It was the only way I felt I could escape reality. As I grew older however, I found that I enjoyed music much more as a soundtrack to life which would serve as ambient noise throughout the day. When the Airpod Pros released, I was excited because for once, you could wear earbuds that could stay in your ear playing music while you could still hear others and have conversation. The implementation of active-noise-cancelling in these earbuds truly changed the way I experience music.
